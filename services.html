Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance for private dining and four to six weeks for larger events. During peak seasons (December, Valentine's week), earlier booking is advised.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our chefs are experienced with all dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-specific menus. We'll work with you to create a bespoke menu that suits every guest.

Is there a minimum spend for private events?

Minimum spend varies by room and day of the week. Our private dining coordinator will provide full details during your consultation, with options to suit a range of budgets.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer video consultations for clients who prefer to plan remotely. We can also arrange a virtual tour of our private dining spaces upon request.
