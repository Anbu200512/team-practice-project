Our Story

Since 1987

A Legacy of Exceptional Dining

La Maison was born from a simple dream — to bring the warmth of French home cooking to every table. Founded by the Dubois family in the heart of Lyon, our restaurant has been a gathering place for food lovers for over three decades.

What started as a small bistro with eight tables has grown into a celebrated destination, earning a Michelin star in 2005 and remaining a beloved institution for locals and travellers alike.

Every dish we serve carries the soul of our heritage — locally sourced ingredients, time-honoured techniques, and an unwavering commitment to quality that has defined us from day one.
